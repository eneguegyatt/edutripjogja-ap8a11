Prambanan Temple is the largest Hindu temple complex in Indonesia, located on the border of Yogyakarta and Central Java. Built in the 9th century, it is dedicated to the Trimurti: Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva. Its magnificent architecture, adorned with intricate reliefs depicting the story of the Ramayana, makes it a UNESCO World Heritage Site. With its historical significance and stunning beauty, Prambanan attracts many visitors from around the world.
